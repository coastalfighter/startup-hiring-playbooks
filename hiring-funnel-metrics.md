# Hiring Funnel Metrics

## Key Metrics to Track

### 1. Sourcing → Screening

* % of candidates responding

---

### 2. Screening → Interview

* % passing initial screening

---

### 3. Interview → Offer

* % selected for offer

---

### 4. Offer → Acceptance

* % accepting offer

---

## Benchmark Targets

* Response Rate: 20–40%
* Interview Conversion: 30–50%
* Offer Acceptance: 70%+

---

## Why This Matters

Without metrics, hiring becomes guesswork.

Tracking these ensures:

* Better decisions
* Faster hiring
* Higher quality hires
