# Startup Hiring Playbooks

Practical hiring frameworks for startups scaling teams from 0 → 100 employees.

---

## What This Covers

* Hiring your first 10 employees
* Building a predictable hiring funnel
* Designing structured interviews
* Creating candidate evaluation systems
* Closing top candidates

---

## Why This Exists

Most founders are terrible at hiring.

They:

* Hire too fast or too slow
* Don’t evaluate properly
* Lose good candidates

This repo simplifies hiring into repeatable systems.

---

## Playbooks

### First 10 Hires

**Focus:**

* Generalists over specialists
* Ownership mindset
* Speed over perfection

**Avoid:**

* Over-hiring senior roles
* Long interview loops

---

### Hiring Funnel

Stages:

1. Sourcing
2. Screening
3. Interview
4. Offer

**Key Metrics:**

* Response rate
* Interview conversion
* Offer acceptance rate

---

### Interview System

* Structured interviews only
* Pre-defined scorecards
* Same questions for all candidates

**Why?**
Reduces bias and improves decision-making.

---

### Candidate Evaluation Framework

Score candidates on:

* Skills
* Communication
* Culture fit
* Growth potential

Use a 1–10 scoring system.

---

### Offer & Closing Strategy

* Move fast
* Sell the vision
* Address objections early

Hiring = Sales.
